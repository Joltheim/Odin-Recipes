Mojito

Ingredients:

  • 10 fresh mint leaves
  • ½ medium lime, cut into 3 wedges, divided
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 1 ½ fluid ounces white rum
  • ½ cup club soda

Description:

"Mojitos are bubbly rum cocktails that taste minty-fresh, citrusy and a little sweet. Club soda stretches out the flavors to create an afternoon-worthy sipper. Mojitos are ultra refreshing on warm days."

Directions:

  1. Place mint leaves & lime wedge into sturdy glass
  2. Use a muddle, releasing mint oils and lime juice.
  3. Add remaining lime wedges and 2 tablespoons sugar.
  4. Muddle again to release the lime juice w/o straining
  5. Fill the glass almost to the top with ice.
  6. Pour in rum and fill the glass with club soda & stir
