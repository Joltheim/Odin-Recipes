Amaretto Sour

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ fluid ounces amaretto
  • ¾ fluid ounce lemon juice
  • 1 cup ice
  • 1 orange slice

Description:

"The flavor profile of an Amaretto sour is the perfect blend of sweet and sour (hence the name). The Amaretto liqueur gives the drink a sweet, nutty almond flavor, while the citrus gives it that sour pucker."

Directions:

  1. Combine amaretto & lemon juice in shaker.
  2. Add ice and shake until chilled.
  3. Strain into chilled sour glass.
  4. Garnish with a slice of orange.
